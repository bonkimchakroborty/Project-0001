Our Programs

8-Week Skill Blocks

Each block focuses on a specific theme. Players enroll in structured cycles that build progressively — 1 session per week, 60–75 minutes, in groups of 6–8 players.

Ball Mastery & Footwork

Master close control, turns, and quick feet through progressive drills.

Passing & First Touch

Learn proper technique for power, placement, and composure in front of goal.
